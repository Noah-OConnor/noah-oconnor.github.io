About Me

I'm a Technical Designer driven by systems thinking, expressive combat, and the challenge of building scalable, player-focused gameplay. I work fluidly across design and engineering, prototyping mechanics in Unreal or Unity, building tools and abilities in C++/Blueprints or C#, and tuning for clarity, feel, and balance.

My focus is on mechanics that scale. Whether that's a modular ability system, an AI framework that supports variety without bloat, or input systems that feel great across devices. I gravitate toward the intersection of design and technology, where great player experience comes from deeply integrated systems.

I'm especially excited by games and tools that support mastery, emergent depth, and long-term engagement. Whether that means MMO combat tuning, competitive gameplay systems, or the engine-level tech that enables them. If you're building deep systems for passionate players, I'm in.
